Hi, I'm Jessica

Hello! I am a recent graduate of the joint UBC/BCIT Honours Biotechnology program, and a self-taught coder with extensive development experience. Through my time at internships, I found a love for software development. This inspired my study of computer science theory, and motivated me to continue refining my coding skills alongside my degree. I am now working as a software developer at Contextual Genomics.

Outside of coding, I am usually spending my time playing video games, or learning something new. Currently I am learning a new language (Korean), and more advanced CS theory. I eventually plan to include a blog on this site with my progress in these things.

I am also always looking for inspiration for my next creation, or something cool to make at a hackathon. Check out a few things I have worked on below!
